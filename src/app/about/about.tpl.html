About My Places Or: What itch am I scratching?

Ever since Android came out, I wanted to build a geolocation app. Well, I'm finally getting around to doing it.

What's so special about this app?

There are plenty of apps which will show me what is around me. Generic places like museums, restaurants, etc. Google Now is really good for this. What they don't show me are the places *I* want to see: that cool little curio shop in SoHo, the sushi restaurant I heard about in the NY Times, that art show I read about on BoingBoing.

That's what this app does. You type in the interesting places and events you want to visit the next time you're in that area and this app will show you them without the clutter of other apps.
